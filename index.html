Who we are

Dear American, RebuildUSA.today is a heritage focused movement, a hybrid PAC and a socio-political welfare organization that organically arose out of the need to politically engage young Americans and involve first time voters in our political process more than ever. We believe it is possible to RebuildUSAtoday and we believe this is the best timne to do it. We also nelieve the best way to do it is by embracing technology more than we do now. That is why we partnered with The PILGRIMS Alliance Party of the United States (be a PAL) in their campaign to vigorously ReBuildUSAtoday with technology. Upgrade aging infrastructures nationwide. Create smart cities. Deploy ambience technology and ambience intelligence all over the United States. And also to get every single American to embrace the new age of American energy. As at now, no single “builder” exists; instead, RebuildUSA.today is made up of individual groups (Radical Republicans, Democrats, Independents, Libertarians, Greens and Working Families party) who share common values about repurposing America, building America and reshaping America’s values – with respect for security, the original meaning of the Constitution, fiscal responsibility, and most of all returning political power to the will of the American people. Main goal is to seek out, provide support and encourage Veterans to run for 3078 county, state and congressional seats. Enable grassroots support for women, teachers, young graduates, farmers and field a new generation of young, dynamic Americans from all backgrounds as candidates at federal, state local and county elections. So if you are an American, a Veteran, a Sergeant, a Master Sergeant or a proud possessor of a Presidential Warrant, please step forward and sign up with us - to run for Congress or sponsor other Veterans who chose to run for office on our ticket. You can also volunteer or donate to help us in our quest to engage and to serve the American people. And don’t forget to make it a duty to inform fellow friends, family, colleagues and co-workers about this campaign.
